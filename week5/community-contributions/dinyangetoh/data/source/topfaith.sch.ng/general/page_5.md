Senior Secondary Section (Years 10 - 12)
The Senior Secondary Curriculum prepares students for the West African Senior School Certificate (WASSC), the Nigerian Senior National Examination Council Certificate (NECO) and the '0" Level University of Cambrige International GCE. Students are counselled and guided to identify their career paths based on capabilities and interests. Subjects groupings as in the table below are therefore maintained to capture a variety of career paths with concious compliance with the national curriculum.
