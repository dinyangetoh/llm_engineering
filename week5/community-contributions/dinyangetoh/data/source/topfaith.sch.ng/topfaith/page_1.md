Our Academic Programmes
International University  Foundation Programme (IUFP)
The IUFP is  a one year Pre-Degree University Foundation Course. It is a fast track alternative to GCE ‘A’ Levels which can be offered by Secondary Schools or 6th Form Colleges. Successful IUFP students can progress to Universities in Australia, Canada, Mauritius, UAE (Dubai) and the United Kingdom to study a range of degree courses that includes Accounting, Bio-Medical Sciences, Business, Computing, Economics, Engineering, Finance, Law, Marketing, Management, Nursing and Social Sciences, just to mention a few
