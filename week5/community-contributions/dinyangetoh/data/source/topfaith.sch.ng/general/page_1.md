Topfaith Legacy College (TLC)
Topfaith Legacy College is located at Ibiakpan, Ikot Ekpene, Akwa Ibom state. TLC provides state of the art facilities, academics, robust environment, and staffing in line with Topfaith's tradition of excellence and total quality education.
Although a non-boarding college, students are exposed to dedicated academic, disciplined and civic life.
The Topfaith Legacy college concept gives parents ample opportunity to be very engaging and interactive in the day to day processes of their wards' academic progress; thus directly and timeously complementing the school's activities.
The excellent innovative services provided include engaging the students from 7:30 am to 5:00 pm in well-structured school activities (Monday to Friday) and provide lunch daily.
Admission Requirements
For Admission into Junior Secondary One:
Pupils must be between 10 and 11 years of age and in their final primary school years.
