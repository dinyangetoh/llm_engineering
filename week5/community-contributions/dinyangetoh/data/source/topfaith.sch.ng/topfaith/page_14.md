Junior Secondary Section(Years 7 - 9)
All subjects contained in the National Curriculum of the Nigerian National Policy on Education are taught in the School for years 7 to 9 (JS 1 to JS 3) to prepare students for the NECO Junior Secondary Certificate Examination and the Akwa Ibom State of Nigeria Junior Secondary Certificate Examination. The subjects are: English Studies, Literature in English, Social Studies, Basic Science (Chemistry, Physics, Biology), Business Studies, Book-Keeping & Accounts, Mathematics, Agricultural Science, French, Basic Technology, Computer Studies, Physical & Health Education, Home Economics, Christian Religious Studies, Fine Arts, Music and Citizenship Education.
